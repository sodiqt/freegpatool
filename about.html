About FreeGPATool

FreeGPATool exists for one reason: students shouldn't have to do GPA math by hand, guess at a conversion, or pay for a calculator they'll use for five minutes a semester.

What we build

Every calculator on this site — GPA, CGPA, final grade, and GPA-to-percentage — runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you type is sent to a server, which is also why your data disappears if you clear your browser storage rather than living in an account somewhere.

How we keep the site free

FreeGPATool is supported by a small number of unobtrusive display ads, not subscriptions or accounts. We deliberately keep ad placement light so it doesn't get in the way of the tools themselves.

Our editorial approach

  • Worked examples over theory. Every guide includes at least one full calculation you can follow along with, not just an abstract formula.
  • Country-specific accuracy. We call out where grading scales genuinely differ — US, UK, Canada, Australia, Nigeria, India — rather than assuming one global standard.
  • Honesty about approximations. Where a conversion (like GPA to percentage) is inherently approximate, we say so directly instead of presenting it as exact.
