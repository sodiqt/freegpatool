Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what information FreeGPATool ("we", "our", "the site") collects when you use our GPA and grade calculators, and how that information is used.

Information stored in your browser

Course names, grades, and credits you enter into our calculators are saved using your browser's local storage so your work isn't lost between visits. This data:

  • Stays on your device — it is never transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • Can be cleared at any time using the "Clear saved data" option in the calculator, or by clearing your browser's site data
  • Is not accessible to us, other users, or any third party

Advertising and cookies

FreeGPATool is supported by third-party advertising, currently served through Adsterra. These ad networks may use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. We do not control the specific data these networks collect; please refer to your ad network's own privacy policy for details on their practices.

Analytics

We may use standard web analytics tools to understand aggregate traffic patterns, such as which pages are most visited. This data is anonymized and used only to improve the site's content and performance.

Children's privacy

FreeGPATool is intended for general educational use by students of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Because the site does not require registration and course data stays in your browser, no personal profile is ever created.

Your choices

You can use FreeGPATool without providing any personal information. If you'd prefer not to be served personalized ads, you can adjust ad settings through your browser or device, or use ad network opt-out tools where available.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@freegpatool.com or via our contact page.
